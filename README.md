📦 Product Distribution Analysis Dashboard

1. Project Title / Headline

📊 Product Distribution Analysis Dashboard

A dynamic and interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze product sales, order distribution, and profitability across multiple states and cities in India—helping businesses track performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

---

2. Short Description / Purpose

The Product Distribution Analysis Dashboard is a visually engaging Power BI report designed to help users monitor sales performance, order volumes, and profit distribution across different regions. The dashboard focuses on highlighting state-wise and city-wise business performance, making it useful for sales managers, business analysts, and decision-makers seeking actionable insights.

---

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:<br>

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.<br>
• 📂 Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning layer for preparing and shaping the dataset.<br>
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, and business metrics.<br>
• 📝 Data Modeling – Relationships established between sales, product, city, and state data for seamless analysis.<br>
• 📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

---

4. Data Source

Source: Product Sales & Distribution Dataset.

The dataset contains information related to product sales, orders, profits, cities, and states. It enables analysis of business performance across multiple regions and provides insights into sales trends, order distribution, and profitability.

---

5. Features / Highlights

• Business Problem

Businesses often generate large volumes of sales data across different locations, making it difficult to quickly identify top-performing regions and profitable markets.

Key questions such as:

- Which city generates the highest sales?
- Which state contributes the most orders?
- Where is the business earning the highest profit?
- How do product costs compare to generated profits?
- Which regions require more strategic focus?

...are difficult to answer using raw transactional data alone.

• Goal of the Dashboard

To deliver an interactive analytical tool that:

- Monitors overall business performance.
- Tracks sales and order distribution across regions.
- Identifies profitable states and cities.
- Compares product costs against profit.
- Supports data-driven business and distribution decisions.

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

• Key KPIs (Left Panel)

Provides a quick snapshot of business performance:

- Total States: 5
- Total Sales: ₹98,369,438
- Total Products: 10
- Total Orders: 100
- Total Profit: ₹21,575

• State Filter Panel

Interactive slicers allow users to filter dashboard insights by:

- Karnataka
- Madhya Pradesh
- Maharashtra
- Telangana
- Uttar Pradesh

• Total Sales by Cities (Bar Chart)

Displays city-wise sales performance and highlights top revenue-generating cities such as Bhopal, Hyderabad, Mathura, Bengaluru, and Pune.

• Total Orders by State (Treemap)

Visualizes the distribution of orders across states, enabling quick comparison of order volume and market demand.

• Total Profit by State (Column Chart)

Shows profit contribution from each state, helping identify the most profitable business regions.

• Product Cost vs Profit (Area & Line Chart)

Compares product costs with generated profits to evaluate overall business efficiency and profitability trends.

• Business Impact & Insights

Performance Tracking: Enables quick monitoring of sales, orders, and profit KPIs.

Regional Analysis: Identifies states and cities driving the highest business performance.

Profit Optimization: Highlights profitable markets for better resource allocation.

Strategic Planning: Supports informed decision-making related to sales growth, inventory management, and distribution strategies.
